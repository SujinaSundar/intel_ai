# Problem Statement

Investors and analysts often spend significant time collecting information from multiple sources such as stock market websites, financial news portals, earnings reports, and company filings. The information is fragmented, making it difficult to perform comprehensive company research efficiently.

The objective of this project is to build **intel.AI – NIFTY 50 Trading Research Agent**, an AI-powered financial research platform that automates data collection, sentiment analysis, information retrieval, risk assessment, and report generation. The platform will consolidate financial insights into a single interface, enabling users to make faster and more informed investment research decisions.
