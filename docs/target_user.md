## C. Target Users

The AI-Powered Investment Research Agent is designed for users who require quick, reliable, and data-driven investment insights.

### 1. Retail Investors

Retail investors often spend significant time gathering information from multiple sources before making investment decisions. This platform provides consolidated stock data, company news, sentiment analysis, and risk assessments to support informed decision-making.

### 2. Financial Analysts

Financial analysts require access to large volumes of financial and market data. The platform automates data collection and analysis, reducing manual effort and improving research efficiency.

### 3. Portfolio Managers

Portfolio managers need continuous monitoring of company performance, market sentiment, and risk indicators. The system provides automated insights that support portfolio evaluation and risk management.

### 4. Research Teams

Research teams can utilize the platform as a centralized repository for stock market information, news intelligence, and analytical insights, enabling more efficient collaboration and research activities.

### User Benefits

* Reduced manual research effort
* Faster access to market information
* Centralized data storage and analysis
* Improved investment decision support
* Automated monitoring of market developments
